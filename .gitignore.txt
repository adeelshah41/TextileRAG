hybrid_score = (1 — alpha) * sparse_score + alpha * dense_score

hybrid_score is the resulting score after combining hybrid and vector search.
sparse_score is derived from keyword-based relevance in the hybrid search.
dense_score is obtained from vector-based relevance.
alpha controls the balance, with values between 0 and 1:
alpha = 1 emphasizes vector search (dense relevance).
alpha = 0 emphasizes keyword search (sparse relevance).
